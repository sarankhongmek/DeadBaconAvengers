Cyber Security

About

Cyber Security is the process of securing networks, devices and software from malicious attackers known as hackers. Hackers are individuals or groups who attack networks for personal gain or to further the motives of an even larger group.

The world is taking large steps towards digitising our society, whereas others would argue we’re already fully there. With our reliance on networks, devices, software and hardware in this era, we need proper defences against those who would harm others physically or otherwise for their personal or political gain.

It’s important that people become more aware about the dangers out there on the internet. The potential threats and how to spot the most common issues. Security on the internet is just as much a standard user’s responsibility as it is a organizations responsibility. Reading through this website should, potentially, better equip you and your family against the very real threat of digital vulnerabilities.

Types Of Attacks

DNS poisoning

DNS spoofing, also referred to as DNS cache poisoning, is a form of computer security hacking in which corrupt Domain Name System data.

SQL Injections

SQL injection is a code injection technique, used to attack data-driven applications.

Spyware

Spyware is software that aims to gather information about a person or organization without their knowledge.

Adware

Adware, is unwanted software designed to throw advertisements up on your screen.
