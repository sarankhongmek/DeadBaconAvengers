Cookie Exploitation

Cookie Poisoning

A problem with Cookies is that the information contained within them can be information the server needs. Without proper authentication, an attacker could change the value of an items price when being sent back to the server, therefore undercharging the attacker for the item.

Thankfully, the practice of storing prices in cookies or server-side information has long since been replaced and therefore is not as widely problematic.
