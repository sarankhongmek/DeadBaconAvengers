Cookie Exploitation

How do you exploit a Cookie?

The information a cookie can hold may not seem like much, but one cookie can hold sensitive information like your username! It all comes down to how a website uses the cookies. Over a HTTP connection, anyone can intercept these cookies with a packet sniffer and read the valuable information contained within. Whereas HTTPS provides encryption, but you’re not totally safe! If someone still manages to get the cookie, they’ll still have the information.
